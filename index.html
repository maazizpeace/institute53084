About Us

M A Aziz Peace Institute of Technology (Institute Code: 53084), located in Gazipur, Bangladesh, is a BTEB-Affiliated technical institution committed to delivering quality education and hands-on industrial training.

Operating under the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB), we offer National Skill Standard Basic (NSSB) 6-month programs that prepare students for real-world employment in technical fields.

Our Indigenous Technology Research and Development Laboratory (ITRD Lab) goes beyond classroom learning — it is a fully equipped research, development, and training facility offering specialized industrial attachment programs in automation, microcontroller design, and electrical engineering.
